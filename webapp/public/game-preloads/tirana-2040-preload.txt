London 1990 preload bundle (tekst)

Narrative bundle and asset placeholders for the London 1990 Baker Street mini-game.
This text-only package enables prefetching without binaries; it is replaced with the WebGL build once ready.

- City layout, lighting loads, and camera angles for the 1990s West End neighbourhood slice.
- Control hooks for mobile gestures and avatar movement along Baker Street and Oxford Street corridors.
- Mission/tutorial descriptions for player onboarding with London emergency services still active.
