## Scoring
- Reds are worth 1 point each.
- Colors range from 2 to 7 points.

## Foul values
Most fouls award at least 4 points to the opponent, or the value of the ball involved if higher.

## Typical fouls
- Hitting the wrong ball first.
- Potting the cue ball.
- No ball contacting a cushion after impact when required.
