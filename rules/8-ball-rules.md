## Objective
Pocket all balls from your assigned group (solids or stripes), then legally pocket the 8-ball.

## Turn basics
- You continue shooting after a legal pocket.
- A foul usually passes turn to the opponent.

## Common fouls
- No ball is hit first.
- Cue ball scratched (pocketed).
- 8-ball pocketed early.
