# Chess Battle Royal firearm SFX

The firearm GLTF model sources currently used by Chess Battle Royal do not ship source-authored audio tracks. To keep binary MP3 files out of the repository and pull request, the game streams these fallback Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International effects directly from their original source URLs at runtime.

| Weapon profile | Sound | Attribution | Runtime URL |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Pistols, revolvers, SMGs, rifles, DMRs, snipers | Gunshot Sound Effect | Alexander / Orange Free Sounds, CC BY 4.0, https://orangefreesounds.com/gunshot-sound-effect/ | https://www.orangefreesounds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Gunshot-sound-effect.mp3 |
| Shotguns | Shotgun Sound | Alexander / Orange Free Sounds, CC BY 4.0, https://orangefreesounds.com/shotgun-sound/ | https://www.orangefreesounds.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/Shotgun-sound.mp3 |
| Grenade launchers / heavy launchers | Cannon Sound Effect | Alexander / Orange Free Sounds, CC BY 4.0, https://orangefreesounds.com/cannon-sound-effect/ | https://www.orangefreesounds.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/Cannon-sound-effect.mp3 |
